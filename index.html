04Philosophy
Shipping something on Tuesday will always beat fifty people who meet about it on Thursday.
  1. 01

    Purpose-built tools.

    Hype cycles come and go. Missed calls and 11pm cravings stick around. We pick the unglamorous problems people actually have, build the tool that fixes them, and then get out of the way.

  2. 02

    Speed over ceremony.

    No roadmap theater, no coming-soon page. We ship a working version in weeks, put it in front of real people, and let real use earn the next feature.

  3. 03

    Small on purpose.

    We could hire. We don't. The same hands that build RankLadder, Kaivo, and Ask Kazmo read every support email. Nobody here is more than one conversation away from the thing that's actually broken.

05How we work

Hunch to live, in a few weeks.

  1. Week 0

    Find the pain

    We look for unglamorous problems with a clear daily user and someone willing to pay for the fix. Can't explain it in one sentence? We drop it.

  2. Week 1–3

    Ship v1

    Three weeks, one working tool, real users. No staging, no waiting room.

  3. Month 2–6

    Find the loop

    We watch what people actually do with it. The one habit that compounds gets all our attention. Everything else gets cut.

  4. Year 1+

    Keep it running

    We run the products we build. Operators and capital come in only when growth says so, not before.
